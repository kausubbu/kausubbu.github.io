Kaushik Subramanian

I am a Senior Research Scientist at SonyAI working on the flagship gaming project. I lead a team working on Gran Turismo Sophy.

My interests are in reinforcement learning, deep learning, and machine learning from human feedback. In particular, I like to find ways to take cutting-edge AI from research to practice.

I completed my Ph.D. in computer science at the Georgia Institute of Technology, where I was advised by Prof. Charles Isbell and Prof. Andrea Thomaz. Before that, I completed my M.S. in electrical and computer engineering at Rutgers University, advised by Prof. Michael Littman.
