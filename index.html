I am a Senior Research Scientist at Sony AI working on the Gaming flagship project. For details, take a look at the GT Sophy website and the associated Nature journal paper.

I completed my Ph.D. degree in the Computer Science Department at Georgia Tech in the Robotics and Intelligent Machines Lab with Prof. Charles Isbell and Prof. Andrea Thomaz (now at University of Texas at Austin).

Prior to Georgia Tech, I completed my Masters degree in the ECE Department at Rutgers University where I worked with Prof. Michael Littman in the Real Life Reinforcement Learning Laboratory (RL3).

My research interests are in reinforcement learning, deep learning and interactive systems. My primary area of focus has been on exploration strategies and integrating learning methods with information from humans. I have had experience in applying my methods to both virtual agents and robotic systems.
