  Kaushik Subramanian

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science Department at Georgia Tech working in the Robotics and Intelligent Machines Lab with Prof. Charles Isbell and Prof. Andrea Thomaz (now at University of Texas at Austin).

I completed my Masters degree in the ECE Department at Rutgers University where I worked with Prof. Michael Littman in the Real Life Reinforcement Learning Laboratory (RL3).

My research interests are in machine learning, reinforcement learning, deep learning and interactive systems. My primary area of focus has been to integrate reinforcement learning methods with information from humans. I have had experience in applying my methods to both virtual agents and robotic systems. As part of my doctoral research, I am working on state space exploration strategies and representation learning (using deep learning) for sequential decision making problems.
