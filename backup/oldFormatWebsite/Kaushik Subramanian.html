I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Georgia Tech working in the Robotics and Intelligent Machines Lab with Prof. Charles Isbell and Prof. Andrea Thomaz.

I completed my Masters degree in the ECE Department at Rutgers University where I worked with Prof. Michael Littman in the Real Life Reinforcement Learning Laboratory (RL3).

My research interests are in Reinforcement Learning (RL), Human Computer Interaction and Robotics. In particular I focus on integrating RL with information form humans and more recently representation learning.
